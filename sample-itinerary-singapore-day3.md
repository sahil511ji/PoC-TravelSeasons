# Sample Day Itinerary — Singapore Senior Special, Day 3 of 7

> **Purpose:** This is a sample, realistic day-itinerary text used to test the AI Daily Recap Video pipeline (PoC 1). It has enough narrative beats, visual cues and emotional moments for the AI to extract a 30-second video with voiceover.

## Day plan (timestamped)

### 6:30 AM — Gentle wake-up
WhatsApp message from Lakshmi: *"Good morning everyone. Coach at 9 sharp. Tea + filter coffee already at the breakfast counter."*

### 7:30 AM — Breakfast at hotel
South Indian counter has appam &amp; stew today, plus the usual continental spread. Mr Iyer goes back for thirds. Mrs Sundaram (turning 70 today, doesn't know about the surprise yet) sits with the Mehras.

### 9:00 AM — Lobby muster, board coach
Air-conditioned coach, 22-seater. Lakshmi does a head-count, distributes cool water bottles and individual umbrellas (rain forecast).

### 9:30 — 11:30 AM — Chinatown walking tour (gentle, flat)
- **Pagoda Street** — slow walk, red lanterns overhead
- **Sri Mariamman Temple** — South Indian architecture in the heart of Chinatown surprises everyone
- **Tea Chapter** — group tea tasting, oolong + jasmine. The owner shows the proper pouring technique
- **Pause** at Chinatown Heritage Centre (toilet stop, AC, group rests for 10 minutes)

**Felt moment:** Mrs Iyer points at the temple gopuram and says quietly, *"This could be Mylapore."*

### 12:00 — 1:30 PM — Maxwell Food Centre (hawker centre lunch)
Group splits across stalls. Lakshmi has pre-arranged seats at the centre table.
- **Tian Tian Chicken Rice** — half the group, including the Mehras
- **Tan's Tu Tu Kueh** for sweet — Mrs Banerjee's favourite
- **Veg thali stall** at the corner — Mrs Sundaram, Mrs Pillai, Mr Patel
- Mr Patel tries laksa for the first time — declares it *"like Goa fish curry but better"*
- Lakshmi pre-orders cendol (shaved ice, palm sugar, coconut) for everyone — first-time treat for most

### 1:30 PM — Coach to Gardens by the Bay (15 min)

### 2:00 — 4:00 PM — Gardens by the Bay
- **Cloud Forest Dome** first — cool, misty, gentle ramps. THE moment of the day: group standing under the 35-metre indoor waterfall, faces lit by mist
- **Flower Dome** — orchids, succulents, baobab tree
- **Group photo** at the Cloud Forest waterfall — Lakshmi takes individual portraits too
- **Felt moment:** Mrs Iyer: *"Can we have these orchids back in Madras?"* — Lakshmi notes it for the group's WhatsApp memory

### 4:00 PM — Brief shower (we expected it)
Sheltered at the Gardens cafe, 20 minutes. Group has tea.

### 4:30 — 6:00 PM — Marina Bay Sands SkyPark high tea
- Coach drops at Tower 3 entrance
- Lift to 57th floor (smooth, ear-pop noted)
- High tea with views of the harbour, Singapore Flyer, supertrees from above
- Sunset begins around 6 — golden light over Marina Bay
- Mrs Mehra and her husband hold hands at the railing — Lakshmi quietly takes a photo
- Group selfie at the railing with Marina skyline

### 6:30 PM — Coach to Little India

### 7:00 — 8:30 PM — Dinner at Banana Leaf Apolo
Authentic South Indian thali on banana leaf. The Mumbai contingent is sceptical, then converts.

**The big moment:** As dessert arrives, Lakshmi signals the staff. They wheel out a chocolate cake with sparklers. *"Happy 70th birthday Mrs Sundaram."* Group sings. Mrs Sundaram cries — happy tears. Lakshmi presents her a hand-stitched silk pouch on behalf of Travel Seasons. Three of the women in the group also tear up. Mr Sundaram sits beside his wife, beaming.

### 9:00 PM — Coach back to hotel
Quiet ride. Some are humming the birthday song.

### 9:30 PM — Hotel, goodnights in the lobby
Most retire. The Banerjees and Mehras have a nightcap at the hotel lounge.

## Music suggestion

Soft ambient instrumental. Slow opening, builds at Cloud Forest, peaks at Marina sunset, gentle for the birthday moment, reflective close. Examples:
- Ludovico Einaudi style piano + strings
- Or AR Rahman ambient (e.g. instrumental pieces from *Bombay* OST)
- Or library track tagged "warm / nostalgic / travel"

Do NOT use upbeat pop or energetic tracks — wrong tone for senior audience.

---

## What we expect from a successful video output

- 30-40 seconds total
- 8-12 cuts (each 2-4 seconds)
- Voiceover throughout, music ducked under
- Subtle text overlays for: "Day 3 · Singapore", "Cloud Forest", "Happy 70th, Mrs Sundaram"
- Last 3 seconds: "Travel Seasons" branding card with tomorrow's destination teaser
- 16:9 aspect ratio for shareability (WhatsApp + admin)
- Vertical 9:16 alternate cut for in-app phone view

## What we DO NOT want

- Generic "tour video" feel
- Stock footage that doesn't match what actually happened
- Voiceover that sounds AI-generated or robotic
- Cuts faster than 2 seconds (senior audience needs time to register)
- Anything that overshadows Mrs Sundaram's birthday moment

## How to run this through the PoC

1. Ingest the timestamps + filmable moments into your pipeline
2. Match assets to moments (waterfall video → Cloud Forest section, birthday clip → birthday section)
3. Generate voiceover from the suggested script (or AI-generate from the prose)
4. Compose timeline + render
5. Export both 16:9 and 9:16 versions
6. Submit to the admin review queue at `/v2/admin/video-review.html`

## Open questions for the AI

- Can you maintain the gentle, warm tone throughout — without slipping into "promotional travel video" energy?
- How do you handle the birthday moment — keep it in or skip if no useable clip?
- What's the longest single shot you'd hold (we'd argue: 4 sec for the waterfall)?
- Can you generate Hindi or Tamil voiceover variants from the same script?
